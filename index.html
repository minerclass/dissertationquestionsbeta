Share your experience with generative AI and academic work.

This site provides role-specific pages for adult participants. The questions ask about experiences, decisions, and observations related to generative AI in academic settings. There are no preferred answers, and participants may skip any question.

What to expect

The interview pages are designed to collect both brief structured responses and longer reflections.

How responses are handled

  • Responses remain in the browser until the participant downloads an export.
  • Participants can download a JSON or Markdown file.
  • Audio recording, when available, is optional and browser-local.
  • Exported files should be transferred only through the approved research process.

Participant choice

  • Participation should follow the final consent process.
  • Participants may skip any question.
  • Participants can type, record, or use both when reflection prompts allow it.
  • There are no correct responses; the study is interested in lived experience and professional judgment.

This map shows how the theoretical foundations and outcomes artifacts connect to the central empirical case study.

1. Theoretical Foundation

  • Qualifying Paper & Companions: The completed intellectual history establishing tertiary algorithmicity and the media ecology of learning friction. Links to Final Qualifying Paper Companion, Technologizing of the Word, and Keeper of the Word simulation.
  • Pedagogical Friction Framework: The definitions of noetic, rhetorical, existential, and infrastructural friction, bounded by the equity guardrail. Links to Canonical Glossary and Friction Framework Primer.

2. The Core Bounded Case

  • Chapters 1-3 Proposal: A qualitative-dominant convergent mixed methods case study investigating educator and institutional sensemaking. Links to Proposal Defense Studio, Methodology Alignment Studio, and Mixed Methods Plan.
  • Research Instruments: Role-specific interview and reflection tools designed with privacy-first boundaries. Links to K-12 Educator Tool, Leader Tool, and Student Tool.

3. Prospective Interpretation

  • Outcomes & Action: Simulations and planning artifacts built to stress-test how findings might be responsibly interpreted before analysis begins. Links to Potential Outcomes Artifacts and Outcomes & Action Studio.

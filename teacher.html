Brief Definitions

Use these short definitions for consistency before the teacher begins the instrument.

Productive friction: cognitive effort, ambiguity, revision, or struggle that helps students learn deeply.
Exclusionary friction: barriers that block access, dignity, language, disability support, or fair participation.
Authentic understanding: the student can explain, defend, revise, or extend the work beyond the polished product.
AI assistance: generative tools used for brainstorming, explanation, drafting, editing, summarizing, translation, feedback, or grading support.

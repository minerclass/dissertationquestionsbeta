Consent and Context

This page collects adult teacher responses for a draft dissertation instrument. Use only after final protocol review and IRB approval.

Research caution: teachers may skip any question. Avoid collecting names, school names, or identifiable student information in open responses.
Optional audio recording: Recording is used only with your permission and only to support accurate transcription. Audio stays in this browser until you download it; it is transferred only through the IRB-approved secure process, is accessible only to the researcher, is retained according to the approved retention schedule, and is deleted from this device after verified transfer. You may decline recording and still take part.
All response data remains local until export.

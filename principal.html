Brief Definitions

Use these short definitions for consistency before the institutional leader begins the instrument.

Productive friction: cognitive effort, revision, ambiguity, or struggle that supports durable learning.
Exclusionary friction: barriers that block access, dignity, language, disability support, or fair participation.
Infrastructural friction: policies, schedules, tools, budgets, compliance rules, and accountability pressures that shape classroom practice.
Responsible AI integration: AI use that preserves student thinking, intellectual ownership, privacy, and equitable access.
